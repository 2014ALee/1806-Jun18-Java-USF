Homemade Hemp Milk

Ingredients List

  • 1/4 cup raw hemp seeds
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/4 tsp of sea salt
  • 1-2 tsp maple syrup

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients into a high-speed blender
  2. Blend on high for about 1-2 minutes until smooth
  3. Store in fridge for up to 5 days
