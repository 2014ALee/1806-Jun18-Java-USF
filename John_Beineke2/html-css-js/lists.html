Homemade Hemp Milk

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of raw shelled hemp sweets
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1/4 tsp of sea salt
  • 1-2 tsp of maple syrup

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients into a high-speed blender
  2. Blend on high for about 1-2 minutes until smooth and silky
