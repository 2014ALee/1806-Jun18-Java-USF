Homemade Hemp Milk

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of raw shelled hemp seeds
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1/4 tsp of sea salt
  • 1-2 tbsp of maple syrup

Directions

  1. Add all Ingredients into a high speed blender (water, seeds, salt, syrup)
  2. Blend on high for 1-2 minutes until smooth and milky
  3. store in fridge for up to 5 days
