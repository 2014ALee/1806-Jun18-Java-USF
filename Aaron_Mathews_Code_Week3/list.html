Homemade Hemp Milk

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup - raw shelled hemp seeds
  • 4 cups - water
  • 1/4 tsp - sea salt
  • 1 - 2 tbsp - maple syrup

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender
  2. Blend on high until smooth and milky
  3. Store in fridge for up to 5 days.
