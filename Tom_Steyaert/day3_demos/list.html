Homemade Hemp Milk

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup raw shelled hemp seeds
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/4 tsp of sea salt
  • 1-2 tsp of maple syrup

Directions

  1. add all ingredients to blender
  2. blend on high for 1-2 minutes until smooth and milky
  3. put in fridge
